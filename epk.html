daniels gone

daniels gone is an experimental artist hailing from Durham, NC. Through an amalgamation of genres including but not limited to symphonic, jazz, grindcore, math rock, IDM, and more, he pushes musical boundaries to their sonic extremes and arrives at a unique and compelling sound all his own. daniels gone's pensive production and intimate lyrics reach beyond the confines of their soundscapes and engage the audience in self-reflective conversation -- his tribulations mirrored and laid bare for the listener in each composition.

Inspired in equal parts by artists like Bjork and Tyler, the Creator, daniels gone seeks to challenge and redefine convention past that which has come before. An artist looking towards the future and also fluent in its precedent, he confidently and deftly navigates between both to create something both novel and familiar.

The first 3 songs on the following playlist are unreleased tracks from an upcoming EP titled "mirror". This project seeks to establish the parameters within which I intend to develop my sound and artistic identity for the foreseeable future. The last 3 songs are already released but expand on the range established in the first half of this playlist.
